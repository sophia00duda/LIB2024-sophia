Odelia

Description

Odelia is a young human woman and an assassin. She has sworn to kill Strahd or die trying to end his cruel rule. Odelia has dark brown hair, glowing red eyes, and deathly pale skin. She was killed and turned into a vampire spawn recently, to her horror and disgust. She dresses in gothic clothing in black and red with many layers. She is bitter, creative, unstable, and mournful.

Favourite Things

  • Her silver blades
  • Dressmaking
  • Tea
  • Arson
  • Rats
  • Dancing
