Sera

Description

Sera is a young elf woman and a cleric. She is on a divine mission to end Strahd's rule over Barovia. Sera has long pink hair, brown eyes, and pale skin. She wears soft and flowing clothing. She is selfless, sweet, resolute, and hopeful.

Favourite Things

  • Lunara, Goddess of Dreams
  • Her amulet
  • Cake rolls
  • Stories
  • Honey tea
  • Soft fruits
