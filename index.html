Curse of Strahd: Thursday Spookies

Welcome to Barovia

I see you’ve taken a wrong turn, gotten lost in the woods, or just stepped outside your front door and into the mist. Didn’t think that was weird at all, huh? Well... welcome to Barovia. You’ll see that the mist is everywhere, but don’t get any ideas. Walking into it might’ve brought you here, but it won’t take you out. Haha. Where would the fun be in that? No, you’re stuck, and the moment you arrived… he knew. Who’s he? Oh, don’t worry, he’ll make a dramatic entrance eventually (probably while kidnapping someone), but I’ll spoil it for you anyway. Strahd von Zarovich. The Devil. The Dark Lord. Big bad evil vampire guy. He won’t bother you much personally though. He is far too busy brooding and kissing portraits of his dead lover. He will send plenty of friends your way though: zombies, hags, werewolves, vampire spawn, an unreasonable amount of wolves, tyrants, and the occasional ghost. So try to stay alive, avoid killing each other over bad decisions (like whether you should have burned that house down) and remember... he is always watching. Welcome to Barovia.
