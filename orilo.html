Orilo

Description

Orilo Stone is a middle aged goliath man and guard for hire. He is searching for a way to escape Barovia to get back to his daughter back home. Orilo has brown and grey hair, silver eyes, and grey skin. He is very tall and strong. He wears soft green clothing. He is thoughtful, careful, kind, and protective.

Favourite Things

  • Springtime
  • Dogs
  • The colour green
  • Funky hats
  • Befriending animals
  • His daughter, Odette
