Victor

Description

Victor Von Vale Venheim II is a young aasimar man and a plague doctor. He is searching for a cure to the sickness that devistated his town. Victor has white hair, gold eyes, and pale skin. He is missing his left eye. He has large black raven wings dresses in gothic clothing. He is curious, studious, chaotic, and reckless.

Favourite Things

  • Shiny coins
  • Poisons
  • Ravens
  • Snow
  • Tea
  • His pet winged rat, Abaddon
