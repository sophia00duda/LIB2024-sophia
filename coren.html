Coren

Description

Coren Halst is a young human man and a hero. He is searching for a way to kill the evil lurking in Barovia. Coren has brown hair, green eyes, and lightly tanned skin. He still wears the rough clothing of his farming village back home. He is determined, strong, stubborn, and selfless.

Favourite Things

  • Wood carving
  • Painting
  • Apples
  • His old longsword
  • His buckle sheild
  • His mother back home
